Installation

  1. Unzip the WebToNative-ios.zip file and open the folder (Note : unzip the Application.zip only in mac os and not in windows os)
  2. Click on the webtonative.xcodeproj file or open the project with xcode
  3. In the xcode , navigate to staging --> config.xml
  4. Now replace the "your url here" string with your own website url.
  5. Now navigate to resources --> images.xcassets and click on Appicon
  6. Replace your app icon by dragging your app icon over the existing app icon
  7. Now change the logo.png to your logo in Resources folder
  8. Now click on LaunchStoryBoard and edit your Splash Screen
  9. To change App Name, Navigate to resources --> webtonative-info.plist
  10. Now replace the field "Bundle Name" and "Bundle Display Name" with your app name
  11. Now Click on "run" button and the emulator will open your app
